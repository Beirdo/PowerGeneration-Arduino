Arduino pins (on attached Nano module)

0  - RXD (UART) - IN
1  - TXD (UART) - OUT
2  - /RF_IRQ (EXT INT0) - IN
3  - DTR - OUT
4  - /GPRS_RST - OUT
5  - GPRS_EN - OUT
6  - LCD_PWM (OC0A) - OUT
7  - /LCD_RST - OUT
8  - /LCD_CS - OUT
9  - /RF_CE - OUT
10 - /RF_CS (SS) - OUT
11 - MOSI - OUT
12 - MISO - IN
13 - SCK - OUT
A0 - unused
A1 - unused
A2 - LCD_DC - OUT
A3 - /SD_CS - OUT
A4 - SDA (TWI)
A5 - SCL (TWI)
A6 - ADC_LIGHT - IN
A7 - VBATT - IN
