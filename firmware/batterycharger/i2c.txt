I2C Bus addresses:

0x20 - PCF8574 - Charger I/O expander
0x21 - PCF8574 - Desulfator I/O expander
0x22 - PCF8574 - LiIon Charger I/O expander
0x3D - SSD1306 - LCD Controller (or 3C)
0x40 - INA219A - Monitoring - Vin
0x41 - INA219A - Monitoring - Vbatt1
0x42 - INA219A - Monitoring - Vbatt2
0x43 - INA219A - Monitoring - VLiIon
0x44 - INA219A - Monitoring - +5V
